Anantha giri hills

Location

Visited date:25-10-2023

Experience:

Ananthagiri Hills, located in the Vikarabad district of Telangana, India, is a beautiful and serene hill station that is becoming increasingly popular as a weekend getaway, especially for those residing in Hyderabad and surrounding areas. Known for its lush green forests, rolling hills, and charming streams, Ananthagiri Hills offers a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life
